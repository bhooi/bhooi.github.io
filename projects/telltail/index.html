TellTail: Fast Scoring and Detection of Dense Subgraphs

Overview

TellTail is a probabilistic measure for scoring dense subgraphs, and a fast algorithm for detecting them.

Paper

  • TellTail: Fast Scoring and Detection of Dense Subgraphs.
    Bryan Hooi, Kijung Shin, Hemank Lamba, and Christos Faloutsos.
    Thirty-Fourth AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI)

Code

The source codes used in the paper are available. [Download]

Datasets

The topically curated Twitter datasets used in the paper can be found at http://mlg.ucd.ie/aggregation/.
The remaining graphs can be found on http://konect.uni-koblenz.de/networks/.
